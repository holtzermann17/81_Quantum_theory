supercategory of variable molecular sets


Definition 0.1.

A supercategory of variable molecular sets (or molecular set variables), §m⁢s⁢v is defined as the supercategory whose class of objects is the class of all possible variable molecular sets, m⁢s⁢v’s, (or molecular set variables), and whose class of morphisms is the class of all molecular transformations, ηt, of m⁢s⁢v’s.

Remark All such m⁢s⁢v–supercategories are semantic interpretations of the ETAS axioms (syntax). This is a mathematical representation of chemical reaction systems in terms of molecular sets that vary with time (or m⁢s⁢v’s, and their transformations as a result of diffusion, collisions, and chemical reactions.
