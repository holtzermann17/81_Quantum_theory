Definition 0.1.

A quantum nano-automaton or (quantum nano-computer) is defined as a microphysical nano-device represented by a quantum automaton that is also capable of quantum computation involving, for example, a field of computations specified over a quantum groupoid. The latter can be realized as a locally compact (topological) groupoid, topological semi-group;in the more general case, it can be defined over a quantum algebraic category, quantum algebroid or a higher dimensional algebraic structure (as previously defined in higher dimensional algebra).

Note: Quantum nano-automata possess intrinsic, extended quantum symmetries defined by quantum groupoid or quantum algebroid representations
